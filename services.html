Connectivity Solutions

Satellite, WiFi & Radio Frequency Communications

IICT delivers comprehensive connectivity solutions that keep your business connected, no matter where you are in Nigeria. Our suite of communication services ensures reliable, high-speed connectivity for urban centers and remote locations alike.

Satellite Communications

Leading suppliers of 70/140 MHz C-and Ku-Band RF transceivers, L-Band if C- and Ku-Band Block up Converters (BUC) and high power solid State Power Amplifiers (SSPA). Perfect for oil & gas, banking, and enterprise communications.

Wireless Community (WiiComm)

Centralized internet connectivity for enclosed communities like estates, residential areas, and organizational premises. We blueprint Internet connectivity by centralized integration of respective living areas.

Radio Frequency Communication

Complete communications solutions including voice, fax, email, telephone interconnect, GPS tracking, and data services for modern business requirements.

Microwave & RF Links

Deployment of Narrowband Digital Microwave Radios for short, medium and long haul terrestrial links with full Network Management capabilities.

VPN & Wide Area Networks (WAN)

Seamless interconnection between headquarters and branch offices using scalable VSAT (1.2, 1.8, 2.4, 3.8 metre) equipment, microwave & RF deployments.

Tri-Fold Scalable VSAT Antenna

Ideal for organizations with field operations (construction firms, geological research, mobile projects). Ensures real-time data and communication between remote sites and headquarters.

Access Control

Biometric Authentication & Access Control Systems

Say goodbye to lost keys and forgotten passwords. IICT's biometric solutions provide unparalleled security and convenience for homes, offices, and institutions.

Fingerprint Recognition

High-precision fingerprint scanners with fast recognition (less than 1 second). Stores thousands of fingerprints with anti-spoofing technology.

Facial Recognition

Contactless access control using advanced facial recognition. Works in various lighting conditions with mask detection capabilities.

Iris Recognition

Highest level of biometric security using unique iris patterns. Ideal for high-security areas like data centers, laboratories, and government facilities.

Keyless Entry Systems

Complete door access control with audit trails, time scheduling, and remote management. Integration with existing door hardware.

Why choose IICT biometric systems? Our solutions eliminate the need for keys, cards, or passwords — your fingerprint becomes your key. With advanced authentication and encrypted data management, we guarantee unmatched safety, privacy, and ease of access.
